This website is made for people who started playing this game or are already amateurs but are overwhelmed by the amount of weapons in the game. They would try their best to play in the game but often fail to do good because poor weapon and attachment choice. We would create this website that would include all of the weapons in the game. The only things not included are the melee weapons. For each weapon we would include the basic information of each gun, like the name, the rank, and RoF (Rate of fire) of the weapon. We are making this website so people can learn about the game better to have more fun and have something to do in their free-time. Players who look at this website would see the best weapons in each class and some of the extra information about the best weapons. This extra info might let the player know how to use the weapon better in what kinds of maps and situations.
